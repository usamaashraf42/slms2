Admission Calendar

Each month Lausanne offers both informational and activity-oriented events designed to provide families greater insight into Lausanne’s successful college preparatory program, global community and the meaningful relationships we’ve built here.

You are also always welcome to schedule an individual tour or student visit day by contacting admission@lausanneschool.com or 901-474-1030.

Middle School Preview & Student Visit Day

Bring your family to an exciting morning with something for everyone! We will offer student visit days in addition to parent tours for students interested in 5-8 grades. We will highlight Lausanne's International Baccalaureate curriculum and how our global community and core values shape our students into empathetic and life-long learning. These events will take place in our Elder Performing Arts Center. Student visits will begin at 8:15 a.m. and last through the day (3:30 p.m.) A brief parent overview will begin at 8:30 a.m. with a tour of your division of interest directly following. To register your family or student, please feel free to visit the link below and click on your division. https://www.lausanneschool.com/plan-a-visit
Date and Time
Tuesday, Oct 1, 2019
8:00 AM - 10:30 AM
Location
Blackmon Gallery
